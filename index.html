Coffee and Me

We are located at the centre of the town in Kinsale, County Cork, Ireland. Cafe21 is a cosy coffee shop serving delicious organic coffee and fresh cui-sine. Coffee is our first passion and foods is second. We have a passion for great taste and healthy ingredients.


We source our beans from sustainable farms and tip the farmers and we use local’s supplier for all our foods as a return to the community.


More Coffee, More Words

As a book’s lover, we have organized a book club in the cafe. Our members will have a meeting every Thursday from 15:00pm to 17:00pm, for hanging around. We have a library corner here where you can choose a book to read, or you can borrow it home. We accept donation and exchange of variety topics of books.
